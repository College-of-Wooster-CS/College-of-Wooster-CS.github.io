Research & Internships

  • Research

    • Applied Methods and Research Experience (AMRE) - join a group of students over the summer and consult for a business, industry, or agency client to solve real-world problems with help from a faculty mentor
    • Sophomore Research Program - work with a faculty advisor on a specific research project during the fall, spring, or summer
    • U.S. National Science Foundation Research Experiences for Undergraduates (NSF REU) - find off-campus summer research opportunities

  • Internships

    • Finding Internships - search with help from the college's Advising, Planning, and Experiential Learning Center (APEX)
    • APEX Fellowships - get paid for unpaid or underpaid summer internships
    • MCS Students team - read about the latest opportunities
    • Internships for Credit - earn course credit for an internship
    • MLH Fellowship - 12-week remote, paid open-source experience
    • These repositories post new opportunities daily and link directly to applications:
      • GitHub - Summer 2026 Internships
      • GitHub - New Grad Positions
      • Off-Season Jobs (co-ops, etc.)
      • Y Combinator startup internships/jobs
    • Other job boards
      • Jobright - has some postings that aren't listed elsewhere
      • Glassdoor Community - underrated resource for opportunities
